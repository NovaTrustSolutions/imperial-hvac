# Design System: Imperial HVAC
**Project ID:** 2015584001604906106

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design follows a "Premium Industrial Chic" philosophy. It is dark, high-contrast, and technical, evoking a sense of engineering precision. The layout is non-traditional, using offset grids and overlapping elements to avoid a "cookie-cutter" look.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Deep Space Charcoal (#1A1A1A):** Primary background color, providing a sophisticated, dark canvas.
* **Electric Cobalt (#0055FF):** Primary accent color, used for high-visibility Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons and interactive elements.
* **Brushed Silver (#E0E0E0):** Secondary accent for technical iconography, secondary text, and borders.
* **Blueprint White (#FFFFFF):** Primary text color for maximum legibility against the dark background.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Headings:** Bold, technical sans-serif (Space Grotesk), emphasizing engineering and blueprints.
* **Body:** Clean, minimal sans-serif, optimized for readability with generous line-height.
* **Accents:** Monospaced or technical labels for a "blueprint" feel.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Electric Cobalt (#0055FF) background, slightly rounded corners (Round 8), with bold white text. High-contrast hover states.
* **Cards/Containers:** Dark-themed with subtle metallic gradients and razor-thin silver borders. Uses overlapping layers for depth.
* **Navigation:** Semi-transparent "Glassmorphism" header with background blur.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Whitespace:** Generous but intentional spacing to highlight architectural imagery.
* **Grids:** Offset and non-traditional alignment.
* **Elevation:** Minimal shadows, using color shifts and overlapping layers to create a sense of mechanical depth.
